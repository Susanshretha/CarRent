Car Rental Agent

As a Car Rental Agent, you'll be the friendly face of our company, ensuring each customer has a fantastic experience. Your duties include processing rental agreements, handling payments, and explaining our vehicle options and policies clearly. You'll inspect cars before and after rentals, addressing any issues professionally.
